Site Name

Our Farm

Site Purpose with Goals

What do you do with the extra fruit or vegetables you grow but can't eat before they go bad? I have built a simple website that can help you enjoy the FRUITS (or vegetables) of your labor. You can simply add what produce you have along with a short description and a picture and set meeting times before sharing it with others within pre-set geographical boundaries. Whether you are looking to make a little extra money from the extra food you grow or give recently harvested produce away so it doesn't go to waste my site is a quick and easy way to help you and your neighbors out.

Target Audience and Scenarios

The target audience is:

  • Small Farmers
  • Homeowners
  • Families
  • Event Planners

Scenarios:

  • Small farm with extra produce not sold through their traditional channel.
  • Homeowner with a produce garden that grows more than they can consume.
  • Families looking for locally grown produce.
  • Groups planning events that want produce grown close to home.
