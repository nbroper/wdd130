Site Name

Our Farm

Site Purpose with Goals

Do you know where your food comes from? Do you know how it is grown and the people that grow it? The answer is probably not but that can change. We are a local farm that grows a wide variety of produce and beautiful flowers who is also dedicated to building meaninful relationships with our customers. We provide many classes and seminars to empower you to begin growing in your own garden.

Target Audience and Scenarios

The target audience is:

  • Homeowners
  • Families
  • Event Planners

Scenarios:

  • A family that is trying to be more efficient with their vegetable garden.
  • Families looking for locally grown produce.
  • Groups planning events that want produce grown close to home.
  • Families or friends wanting to have events or pictures at a farm location.
