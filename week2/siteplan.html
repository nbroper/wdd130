Site Name

Muddy River Adventures - The wild wild west of rafting adventures.

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of the website is to provide information on river rafting in and near Moab Utah on the Colorado and Green Rivers. We also expect to add a reservation system including and interface with a shopping cart checkout capability. We will have company logo'd merchandise available to purchase as well as photos of tour groups to order after the adventures have taken place. We want to provide detailed information on when trips are available and for what price that is easy to locate on the site. We will also include detailed information on what to bring with you and what to expect while on the river. We will have reviews from previous patrons that highlight the adventure and rapids. A main goal of this site is to provide easy to access information for people who are planning their trips in advance or are trying to find availability during their planned trips.

Target Audience and Scenarios

We will be targeting: scout groups, youth groups, off roading clubs, and small and large family groups.

Scenarios:

  • What will it cost for X amount of people for a tour?
  • Can we book a tour on Y date?
  • Can we get a refund if weather is bad?
  • What do we need to bring with us for our rafting trip?
  • How long do rafting trips typically last?
  • How can I order pictures of our rafting adventure?
